PETSHOWS

Handlers set up their dogs for judging so that their stance is perfect when the judge views them this is known as hand stacking.

Cats may also be presented standing freely, in the so-called "self stack" or "free stack".

A Pet Show is a great end of the school year activity. A Pet Show encourages parent involvement and is both fun and educational! Proper planning of the pet show will enhance success. A Stuffed Animal Pet Show can be a good alternative to live animals

Pajama Party

You could hire a hall or throw a pyjama party for pets and owners in your own home. This doesn't need to be overnight but rather a few hours in the evening, encouraging owners and pets to wear fun pyjamas, play games and socializing.

Photo Booth

You could organise a photo booth at your home, at a local park if the weather allows or even at a local fair if you are able to pay to hire space. Being at a fair has the added benefit of catching people passing on foot who might not otherwise be aware of your cause.
